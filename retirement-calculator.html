How Much Will You Have for Retirement in 2026?

This free calculator projects your retirement savings using compound growth. It includes your current savings, regular contributions (employee + employer match), expected returns, and inflation. As of 2026, the average employee contribution is ~9.5% with employers matching ~4.7%.

Practical Tips

Frequently Asked Questions

How is the future value calculated?

Using compound interest on your current savings plus annual contributions (including employer match) over the years until retirement.

What is the safe withdrawal rate?

We use 3.9% (2026 guidance) — you can withdraw this percentage of your portfolio in the first year of retirement, adjusted for inflation annually, with a high chance the money lasts 30+ years.

Are the results guaranteed?

No. Market returns and inflation vary. This is an educational projection only. Consult a financial advisor for personalized planning.
