Rent vs Buy in 2026 — Which is Smarter?

This calculator compares the true long-term cost of renting versus buying, including mortgage payments, property taxes, insurance, maintenance, home appreciation, and rising rent. Current 30-year mortgage rates are around 6.4% as of April 2026.

When Buying Usually Wins

Buying tends to win if you stay 7+ years, can get a good mortgage rate, and the home appreciates well. Renting often wins in high-cost areas or if you value flexibility and low upfront costs.
